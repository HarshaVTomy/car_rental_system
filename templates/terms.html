Rental Terms and Conditions

We don’t take any sort of securities from customers, please make sure you have attached the relevant documents(Photo+Passport Copy and Visa page +Driving License [Indian/International] ) as prescribed in the booking form.

Delivery and Pickup

First time customer – Vehicle will be delivered at your home address(as per document proof) or If Airport pickup is needed driver will pick you up from Airport(can provide you the car from airport itself on fulfilling exact booking with all required documents submitted on the website) and will drop you with the vehicle at your home address(only first time). Payment* of rental period should be made in advance on delivery of vehicle.

Existing customers– Vehicle will be provided before your arrival or at Home address /Airport /Place of your choice.

Our Garage is in Kottayam, Delivery charges from Kottayam Garage and return charges to Kottayam Garage are applicable on rental (Driver Batta + Fuel Charges).

Rental Period and Mileage

A day is calculated as per 24 hours from delivery time of car, minimum 1 day rental is charged on delayed timings.

There is monthly mileage restriction of 4000 kms, if less than a month it will be 130 kms/day. Extra kms over the rental period are chargeable.

NB: For Fortuner, Hycross Hybrid and other Luxury Cars(eg: Audi, BMW, Benz,Jaguar) The daily limit is 100 kms/day
